POSEIDON

ABSTRACT

Project Poseidon addresses the catastrophic 92% loss of UK seagrass and the rising threat of "acoustic smog" in our oceans. As lead scientists, we propose a three-tiered STEM framework—Reseeding, Silent Propulsion, and Molecular Filtration—to restore the UK’s Blue Economy to a carbon-negative state by 2050.

Caring for Nature

01. AI-Driven Seagrass Reforestation

The Crisis: Habitat loss has led to a collapse in fish nurseries. Seahorses and young Bass are losing their primary refuge.

The Fix: We use AI drone swarms to plant Zostera marina. According to Research Pack data, this captures carbon **35x faster** than forests.

02. Magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) Silent Transit

The Crisis: Shipping noise (cavitation) prevents whale communication, leading to beaching and reproductive failure.

The Fix: No propellers. We use magnetic fields and **Green Hydrogen ($H_2$)** to move water silently, protecting the acoustic environment.

03. Ionic Chitosan Filtration

The Crisis: Microplastics building up in the "Trophic Levels" (food chain), poisoning everything from plankton to humans.

The Fix: Using **Chitosan ($C_6H_{11}NO_4$)**—made from recycled seafood shells—to bond with plastic particles at river mouths.
